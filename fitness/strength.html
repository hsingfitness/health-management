Grow Stronger. Look Better.

Build lean muscle through effective training, proper nutrition, and consistent recovery.

Why Build Muscle?

Muscle improves strength, boosts metabolism, supports healthy aging, and enhances confidence and everyday performance.

Benefits

  • Increase muscle mass
  • Improve body composition
  • Boost metabolism
  • Build strength
  • Improve posture
  • Support bone health

Key Exercises

  • Squat
  • Bench Press
  • Deadlift
  • Pull-Up
  • Overhead Press
  • Row

Muscle Building Essentials

  • Train consistently
  • Use progressive overload
  • Eat enough protein
  • Prioritize recovery
  • Sleep 7–9 hours
